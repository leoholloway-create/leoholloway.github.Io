About

What is One Hundred Percent Human?

One Hundred Percent Human is a film studio that creates stories that actaully matter, stories with purpose, made by humans.

Who is behind it?

The studio is led by myself; Leo Holloway, it was created because more than ever now we need humans to make art with purpose.

What is its purpose?

To create films that feel lived-in rather than performed — stories that explore what it means to be human.
