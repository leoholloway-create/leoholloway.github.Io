About

What is One Hundred Percent Human?

One Hundred Percent Human is a film studio focused on emotional realism, memory, and human behaviour.

Who is behind it?

The studio is led by filmmaker Leo Holloway, exploring storytelling through cinematic atmosphere and emotional perspective.

What is its purpose?

To create films that feel lived-in rather than performed — stories that explore what it means to be human.
