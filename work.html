Square eyes.

Square eyes is a film about what happens when you are presented a choice between facing your past or keeping on trying to run away from it. As a cinematographer i explored visual elements how to use visual elements to add more layers to a story for example: I used dim lighting to make the presence of the man in the suit more mysterious and used a dutch angle to make the viewer feel more uneasy when the man in suit was in the shot. My role as a cinematographer was to manipulate how the story was told visually, for example i controlled the film's pace and momentum by using a slow and controlled camera movement.
